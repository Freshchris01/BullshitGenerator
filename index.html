About this App

This Application was created during the Imagine Cup Hackathon in Munich, Germany in 2016.
It creates with the help of Microsoft Cognitive Services a pseudo inspirational motivation image.
im-gen stands for inspirational motivation generator.

Create your own motivation images, download them and share them.
